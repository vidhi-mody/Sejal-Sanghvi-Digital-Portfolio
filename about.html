Sage (1997, Mumbai, India) is a Visual Artist stuck in a Wrong World. Throughout her life, she has been constantly experiencing a lack of belonging and displacement reflected by her surroundings. Her work is associated with the relative concept of reality, truth and mankind. Her strong application of permutations of patterns brings her comfort in this tumultuous existence. Marred by censorship and restriction of intellectual and expressive emancipation, her work is an imagery that posits the loss of logic and evokes questions that rarely have answers.
